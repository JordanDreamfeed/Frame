06 — Notes & Crew
Frame Brunette editorial — rooftop
Shots Moodboard Schedule Notes

Director's notes

Last edit
11 May, 2026

The mood

Pull from late-90s editorial — the camera close, the styling quiet, the light doing most of the work. We want texture and intimacy. Less posed than the last shoot. More candid.

The wardrobe should feel lived-in, not arranged. If something catches the wind, hold the frame and stay on it.

Gear

A7s III, 35mm and 85mm primes, macro for the detail pass. Flag kit and a single bounce. We won't need much fill — golden hour gives us most of what we need on the rooftop.

Logistics

Roof access via the side door at 06:45. Building manager has been told. Please don't loiter on the public stairwell — they care about that.
